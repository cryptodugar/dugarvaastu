Vaastu for FACTORY

Industries should be designed in such a manner that the production of goods should increase & so the movement of finished goods.Vaastu Shastra ensures the Fruitful operation of business supply chain, leading to larger production and better financial profits, Applying Vaastu priniciples for industry can be of great support and referred to it roots way for prosperity, safety, strengths in business.

"We provide Vaastu Consultancy services for all Industry types (Small to Large Scale) located anywhere in the world."

We have over 30 yrs. Of experience in designing/correcting factories as per the principles of Vaastu Shastra.We have designed hundreds of factories across the world which include some of the well-known brands.

While studying our client’s factory we look for (but not limited to) these aspects:-
  • How is the basement constructed – Location, direction, height, depth etc.
  • Beams in Factory – Manager’s compartment, Laborer’s area, production facility etc.
  • Windows in the Factory. Location, direction, Thickness, color, height, width etc.
  • Staircases in factory - Location, direction, Thickness, color, height, width etc.
  • Location of machines – Heavy, Medium and Light
  • Location and direction of Finished Goods.
  • Location and Direction of electrical and electronics equipment’s like A/C, generators etc.
  • Location and direction of tanks – Septic tanks, Overhead water tanks, underground water tanks etc.
  • Location and Direction of Billing Office, Corporates office, Accounting office, toilets etc.


We don’t recommend structural changes or demolition in the property for Vaastu defect correction. We recommend Easy and practical solutions to make your house Vaastu-perfect.
